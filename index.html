About the Program


CTE is an Educational option that provides students the opportunity to explore the world of work through an academic experience that includes increased opportunities for earning industry-valued credentials, college credit, and work-based learning while emphasizing real-world skills and practical knowledge within a selected career focus!

Our CTE students take specialized courses in addition to their required courses while getting the opportunity to participate in internships, engage with mentors and practice what they are learning through hands-on projects.

There is no better time to engage in CTE programs like the latest data from employers, report companies having difficulty filling job openings because of a lack of qualified candidates. A skilled workforce yields a strong economy. The NJSLS career-ready practice standards which we instill in our CTE students reinforces the practices and skills to produce qualified career-ready applicants.
